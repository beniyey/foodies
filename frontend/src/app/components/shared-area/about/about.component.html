About us

Foodis's mission is to make cities better places for customers, merchants and couriers alike.
Foodis's platform makes it easy for customers to order whatever they need on one app,
for merchants to make additional sales, and
for couriers to make meaningful earnings flexibly.
To enable this, Foodis develops a wide range of technologies from local logistics
to retail software and financial solutions, as well as operates its own grocery
stores under the brand Foodis Market.
